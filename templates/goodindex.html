U.S. Public Education Funding

Our Topic

In 2018-2019 almost half a million public school teachers (and educational staff) have protested or participated in strikes across the United States. Many of these protests/strikes were related to the #RedforEd movement. The demands varied by state but highlighted teacherâ€™s dissatisfaction with compensation, funding, working conditions, and support services for students.

This project analyzes U.S. education data to better what led to statewide protests/strikes in 7 states: Arizona, Colorado, Kentucky, North Carolina, Oklahoma, South Carolina, Virginia, and West Virginia. Note: There have also been municipal level protests in California, Georgia, and Tennesse. In order to contain project scope these smaller protests are not included in our analysis.
