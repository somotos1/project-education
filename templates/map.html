U.S. Public School Funding Choropleth Map 2000-2015

Overview and Analysis

This visualization represents the state funding per student enrollment for each U.S. State. The data is presented in 5-year intervals: 2000, 2005, 2010, 2015. You can interact with each state to see the relevant funding per enrollment figure. By clicking on the layers icon on the top right of the page you can change the map layout and toggle between each years data. The states we have identified have increased funding per enrollment each year. Some have also fluctuated indicating higher funding per enrollment states have chosen to strike. While funding is a factor, there are more factors attributable to teacher strikes in the United States.
